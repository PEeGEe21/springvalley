Primary School

Our School strives to provide an appropriate education for each family member's child while meeting the educational needs of a wide range of individual abilities. In order to prepare for junior school, we work to develop the whole person. It is a joy to watch the pupils develop their ability to apply themselves to a variety of activities and tasks in the busy world of school.All of our pupils are able to pursue their interests and talents, making the most of each and every day at this fantastically well-equipped school.
